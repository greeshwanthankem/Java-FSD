Help & FAQ

  • How to register? – Go to Events, fill the form, and click Register.
  • How to find my location? – Click the "Find Nearby Events" button in the portal.
  • How do I contact support? – Email events@localcouncil.com or call the contact given in the footer.
  • How to reset my preferences? – Use "Clear Preferences" in the Save Your Event Preferences section.
